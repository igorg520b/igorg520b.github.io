Abaqus simulations of fracture

Batch 2

The force is applied to the surface nodes located between the angles of 43.4 and 60.0 degrees (where 90 degrees is vertical and 0 degrees is horizontal). The force itself is applied at 40-degree angle. The magnitude of the force is distributed in a bell-shaped way using normal density function with σ=[angle span]/6.

Batch 3

The force is applied at the same angles as in Batch 2, but the magnitude of the force is distributed uniformly.

The (b) version has additional boundary conditions up to the half-height of the block.

The (c) version is the block of length 5.

Batch 5

The force is applied to the nodes between angles 60 and 77 degrees (more vertically than previous batches). The angle of force application is 68 degrees, and the magnitude is distributed uniformly.

The (b) version has additional boundary conditions up to the half-height of the block.
